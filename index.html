Greg LeBlanc

Hi! I've spent over eleven years in the information security industry in various roles. If you're here, you are probably interested in some of *my* interests and skills. Some of my favorite responsibilities have been incident response analyst, intelligence researcher, automation engineer and systems administrator. I've helped build production systems that are currently in use by many internal users, along with tactical scripts that help analysts gain capabilities in moments of need. Currently, I lead a function of 5 people who's goal is to enable internal analysts and researchers to collect, curate and annotate data into intelligence.


At home, I am an extremely lucky father of three wonderful children. I spend the majority of my free time outdoors in the woods camping, hiking or taking our dog for a walk.
